About this Project

The initial idea was to create a web app or website that would allow people to access more books as well as encourage more people to read. We noticed that it is quite difficult to read and listen to music with words at the same time so we wanted to create a site that would expose people to more classical music and give them a resource to use to find music that they can play in the background while they read.

About the CreatorsTina and Bertwocane

One of us really likes reading and the other is very interested in music so we decided to come up with a project that could combine both of our interests.
